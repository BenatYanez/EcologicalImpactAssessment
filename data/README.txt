Data gathered in the Field in Arran and in the Desk Based Survey and the Metadata associated with that data

The Protected Species sheet is a list of species in Scotland that is protected by law and the laws that protect them
The latests version was published in 2022 by Nature Scot:https://www.nature.scot/doc/table-all-scotlands-protected-species

The Scottish Biodiversity List is a list of animals, plants and habitats 
the Scottish Miniters consider of principal importance for biodiversity conservation in Scotland.
The latest version was published in 2020 by Nature Scot:https://www.nature.scot/doc/scottish-biodiversity-list
